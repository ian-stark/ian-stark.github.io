Hi. I’m Ian Stark, a computer scientist at the University of Edinburgh where I am a member of the Laboratory for Foundations of Computer Science and a Senior Lecturer in the School of Informatics. My research is on mathematical models for programming languages and concurrent systems: ranging from computer security in the Morello processor to biochemical modelling with the continuous pi-calculus, proof-carrying code as digital evidence, and category theory for reasoning about name generation.
